About

I have a PhD in Educational Psychology and work in a rural school's factotum, coordinating academic, social/emotional, and behavioral support services, training staff on data-based decision making, how to administer and interpret our assessment systems, and conduct applied research and program evaluation projects.

This blog is for all of the extra data exploration projects I come up with in my spare time. Most likely it will be heavily focused on education topics, but I also really enjoy reading about and exploring research on running, cycling, weightlifting, nutrition, and general wellness.

My website is generated through github and made possible by use of the blogdown package created by Yihui Xie.
